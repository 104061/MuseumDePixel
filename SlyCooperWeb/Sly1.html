Welcome to the Sly Cooper Fan Page!

Your ultimate destination for all things Sly Cooper. Dive into the world of stealth, adventure, and thievery with Sly and his gang!

Explore the Sly Cooper series below! Sly Cooper is a charming and action-packed series that follows the adventures of Sly Cooper, a raccoon thief with a heart of gold, and his loyal team of quirky friends. The series combines stealth, platforming, and puzzle-solving elements, making it a thrilling experience for players of all ages.

Sly, the master thief, comes from a long line of criminal masterminds. However, after discovering that his family’s precious Thievius Raccoonus—a legendary book of thieving techniques—has been stolen, Sly embarks on a mission to recover it and protect his family’s legacy. Alongside him are his two trusty companions: Bentley, the genius turtle and tech expert, and Murray, the lovable, muscle-bound hippo.

Together, they work to take down evil villains, uncover secrets, and steal back what’s rightfully theirs. With its unique blend of stealth and action, Sly Cooper encourages players to sneak around, avoid detection, and pull off epic heists.
Whether it's using Sly’s signature cane for stealthy attacks or mastering different gadgets and moves, the series provides a fresh take on the platformer genre.

The vibrant world of Sly Cooper is rich with humor, exciting levels, and memorable characters, all set in beautifully animated environments. Whether you’re a seasoned gamer or new to the series, Sly Cooper offers a captivating experience that mixes action, adventure, and stealth, making it a must-play for anyone seeking a fun, challenging game with heart.
